Clawdbot: The Space Lobster's Gateway.

Carry your own personal AI assistant in your pocket across WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord.

Clawdbot is a powerful, cross-platform bridge that connects modern AI agents to your favorite messaging apps. It acts as a long-running Gateway that allows you to carry your own personal AI assistant in your pocket.

1. How It Works

At its core, Clawdbot runs a Gateway service. This service owns the connections to your messaging channels and provides a control plane for AI agents. When you send a message on WhatsApp or Telegram, Clawdbot routes it to the agent, which then processes the request and streams the response back to your chat.

2. Key Features

  • Multi-Channel: Integration with WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord, and iMessage.
  • Agent Bridge: Powers the Pi coding agent and the Clawd space-lobster assistant.
  • Context & Memory: Sessions are managed per-sender or per-group, allowing the AI to remember prior context.
  • Media Support: Native handling for images, audio, and documents.
  • Control UI: A browser-based dashboard for configuration, logs, and real-time monitoring.

The Lore

The name Clawdbot is a mashup of CLAW + TARDIS. Born as a "Space Lobster" assistant, it brings personality to AI interactions. Its motto? "EXFOLIATE!" — a friendly, lobster-themed alternative to the Daleks' "EXTERMINATE".

Conclusion

Clawdbot bridges the gap between powerful AI capabilities and the apps you use every day. It's not just a bot; it's an evolving companion.
